REFUND POLICY, TIME LIMITATIONS AND OTHER FEES REQUIRED TO OBTAIN A CONTRACTORS LICENSE

If for any reason the student wishes to cancel, the student may do so by notifying Contractors Intelligence School in writing within 5 business days from the date the package is delivered.  If the student cancels, they must mail unopened study material back to Contractors Intelligence School within 14 calendar days of the delivery at their expense and the student will receive a refund (less the shipping cost and 25% re-stocking fee if package was shipped out).  The refund will be issued within 30 days of the withdrawal.  AFTER 14 BUSINESS DAYS THE STUDENT WILL NOT BE ELIGIBLE FOR A REFUND UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES EXCEPT UNDER THE TERMS OF THE GUARANTEE BELOW.  Contractors Intelligence School agrees to provide the student with updated material for a six month period.  If the student does not complete the course during this time period, the enrollment is void and no refund is due to the student. If it has been less than 1 year, the student may pay a non-refundable $85.00 enrollment extension fee that will extend the enrollment for an additional six month period from the day the extension is paid. If it has been more than one year since the student has enrolled, they will have to pay 50% of the course price to re-enroll.  Other fees NOT included in tuition: SHIPPING AND HANDLING, STATE APPLICATION FEE: $300.00 (Paid directly to the state with the application.), FINGERPRINTING: Approximately $66.00, STATE LICENSE FEE: $180.00 (Paid directly to the state after the student passes the exam.), CONTRACTOR’S BOND: approx. $115 per year.  This bond is required after the student passes the test. If the student is obtaining a C-39 (roofing) or a C-53 (swimming pool) license, the bond may cost considerably more. If the student has had a contractors license suspended or revoked in the past, a disciplinary bond may be required, which is extremely expensive and varies depending on a number of factors. All of these fees are subject to change without notice. Applicant must also have 4 years of experience within the 10 years preceding the application.

GUARANTEE

This is a California contractor's exam preparation course not a trade school. If you are unfamiliar with the trade, the student should seek more training outside of this course.  Contractors Intelligence School guarantees that the student will pass the California contractor's exam, or will refund student’s tuition (subject to the conditions below). 

IN-CLASS EXAM PREPARATION COURSE GUARANTEE POLICY

If the student passes both exams then no refund is due.  If the student fails the exam on the first try, the student must request updates to the study material and attend another class.  If the student fails the exam on the second try, we will cover one-time $60.00 retest fee.  If the student fails the Law exam on the third try, then the refund will be issued for the Law tuition.  If the student fails the Trade exam on the third try, we will issue a full refund for the trade tuition.  In order to receive a refund, the student must request a refund in writing, along with the Notice of Failure To Pass an Examination issued by the state, within 15 business days from the date of Notice of Failure.  The study material must be returned with a request for a refund. This guarantee is void if the student does not take California contractor's exam within 6 months of his/her enrollment.  This guarantee is also void if the student fails to appear for the state exam scheduled.  Approved refund will be sent to the student within 30 days of the date of the request. The guarantee is also void and no refund is due if the student falsifies in his/her State paperwork or if the student cannot prove the required experience for the application.
